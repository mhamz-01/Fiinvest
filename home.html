    Models And Training Methods

    In our machine learning projects, we employ a range of models and training methods tailored to specific tasks. For supervised learning, we often use **decision trees**, **support vector machines**, and **neural networks** for accurate predictions on labeled datasets. In unsupervised learning, techniques like **k-means clustering** and **principal component analysis (PCA)** help uncover hidden patterns. We utilize **cross-validation** to ensure model robustness and avoid overfitting, while **transfer learning** with pre-trained models enhances efficiency and accuracy. Our training process includes fine-tuning hyperparameters and **data augmentation** to create high-performing solutions that address complex challenges across various domains.
